    Our Story

    La Hacienda Products is a family owned dairy business founded and established in 2008. We specialize in the distribution of the best Latin American dairy products in Miami, Florida. When our founders first opened shop, they had one thing in mind: deliver the best Latin American products with the best customer service. We offer a wide variety of cheeses while providing exceptional customer service to our clients. We work with clients throughout the 48 states in the continental United States. Frying cheese, cuajada, blanco cheese, requeson cheese, and smoked cheese are a few of the several delicious Latin American cheeses we carry. Aside from our popular cheeses, we sell additional imported Central American products such as: beans, sodas, and annatto.
